Dutch “King’s Day” Windmill Festival 2017
Embassy of the Netherlands in the United States
Event Description
"Join the celebration of Dutch King's Day in San Francisco! By the Murphy Windmill in Golden Gate Park you'll find children’s games, music, a flea market, a beer garden, bitterballen and more! Entrance is free. (No going Dutch here!)

King’s Day celebrates the birthday of King Willem-Alexander. This year he turns 50! It is the national holiday of the Netherlands, and the country's biggest national event. Everything and everyone turns orange and celebrates. Last year 3,000 Dutchies and Americans joined the biggest celebration of King's Day on the West Coast.

We hope to see you again this year, but you'll have to wear orange!
More information: http://www.sfdutch.com/dutch-in-sf/kings-day.html You can start the day with an Orange Bike Tour, which starts at 10:30 a.m. at the Dutch Windmill in Golden Gate Park. Sign up: http://www.sfbike.org/event/orange-bicycle-ride/ "
