Job Search Essentials: Interview with Confidence
University of California, Berkeley
Event Description
It's no secret: the interview is a make-it or break-it point in every job or internship search. Join us for an interactive workshop where you will learn what to expect in a job interview, best practices for how to prepare, and strategies for answering tough interview questions like a pro.

This program is part of our Job Search Essentials series. Open to students of all majors and class levels!
