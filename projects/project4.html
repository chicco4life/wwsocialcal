Landing a Consulting Job or Internship
University of California Berkeley
Event Description
Thinking about recruiting for consulting next year? Don't miss this chance to hear insider tips from three Haas students who have secured consulting opportunities. By attending this session, you'll have a better idea of what consulting is, whether you are actually interested in the work, and what you need to do over the summer to be ready to recruit next fall.
This is a Q&A session, so come ready to discuss.

**This event targets Haas students, but is open to all UC Berkeley students. Attendance will be allowed on a first come, first served basis.

FYI - There is another consulting workshop on Thursday, April 20 from 1-2:30pm (see: berkeley.joinhandshake.com/events/48841) that will offer information on the consulting field from an employer's perspective. Be sure to attend this session as well for the opportunity to network with Capgemini Consulting (who will be on campus next fall to recruit!).
