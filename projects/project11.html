"Come this Tuesday and be THE FIRST to watch two episodes of Netflix’s adaptation of the widely acclaimed movie “Dear White People”!

Dear White People follows a group of students of color facing white privilege on campus at a predominantly white Ivy League college. With its sharp sense of humor, the series tackles issues regarding race and diversity (or the lack thereof). And seeing that looking for love, searching for your identity, and trying to find your path are all struggles that college students go through, Dear White People does a great job at providing a Black perspective of satirizing race in America.
