BAM FEST 2017
BAM PFA - Student Committee

"The BAMPFA Student Committee is excited to present BAMFEST, our annual Berkeley arts & music festival. Join us on the Crescent Lawn for an evening of live music, diy crafts, interactive art, cozy couches, screenprinting, student artwork, pin-making, and so much more!
