Free LSAT Seminar at UC Berkeley
Blueprint LSAT Preparation
Event Description
Join us on Tuesday, April 25th at 7:00PM at UC Berkeley - Barrows Hall, Room 20.

Phi Alpha Delta and Blueprint LSAT Prep are hosting a free workshop about the Law School Admissions Test (LSAT).

Blueprint's LSAT guru, Lianna McSwain, will cover the importance of the test in the law school admissions process. As well as go over scoring, sample problems, the correlation between LSAT scores and future salary, and much more!

This seminar will benefit students at any point in their collegiate careers. So be sure to take advantage of this opportunity to gain some insight on all things LSAT related.

Interested in attending this event? Be sure to click "Find Tickets", to secure your spot and to receive your discount for Blueprint's classroom course!
