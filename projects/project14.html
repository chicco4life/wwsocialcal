"PSE x AKPsi presents: ☀PLAYTIME WITH PUPPERS☀

Come out to Sproul on April 26th between 11AM-3PM to play with the cutest puppers and doggos you've ever seen! All the proceeds will go towards Copper's Dream.

▶ PRICE: $2 FOR 5 MIN OF PUPPER LOVE (Additional donations are accepted!)
▶ PAY IN CASH OR VENMO @jonathan-duan --

Copper's Dream is an all-breed dog rescue based in the San Francisco Bay Area. All of their dogs live in foster homes until adopted - they do not own a physical shelter. This year, Copper's Dream has already organized 156 adoptions, and have organized a total of 3,990 adoptions since their establishment.
