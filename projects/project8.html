2017 Cal VRathon: Coding and Graphic Design Competition
Fulldive
Event Description
Join us at 2017 Cal VRathon: Coding and Graphic Design Competition and learn about virtual reality, immersive technology and more!

Sign up through Eventbrite: https://www.eventbrite.com/e/2017-cal-hackathon-coding-and-graphic-design-competition-keynote-speech-with-htc-ceo-sponsored-by-tickets-33420432413

Top three winning teams will receive following prizes:
1st Place: $500 Cash + $800 worth HTC VR Headset
2nd Place: $300 Cash
3rd Place: $100 Cash


The event includes two major parts:

One major part is a speaker conference where Cher Wang, CEO of HTC (and many other leaders in VR industry) will share with us their visions for virtual reality. The speaker event aims to create more new ideas and at the same time give students immersive experiences and education about Virtual Reality

The other part is a hackathon session where students with diverse backgrounds (graphic design, engineering, etc) who are interested in VR work collaboratively in teams on developing VR graphic design software solutions.

You may choose to attend both the speaker conference and hackathon, or just one of the above, depending on personal preference.

This is a wonderful opportunity for attendees to meet with other VR engineers, companies, and learn about front-end VR software and hardware technology by interacting with leaders in the industry.

Food and drinks will be provided*
