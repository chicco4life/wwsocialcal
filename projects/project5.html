Cornerstone Research Case Competition Kick-Off
University of California Berkeley
Event Description
Cornerstone Research, in partnership with Voyager Consulting, is excited to announce the 2017 spring semester Economic Consulting Case Competition. This is an opportunity for UC Berkeley students to showcase their programming skills, expertise in econometric techniques, and economic intuition. The Case Competition will begin with a kickoff event on April 17th in the Career Center's Blue & Gold Room. Final presentations will be held on Friday, April 28th. Please see the attached flyer for specifics and logistical details.

Attire: Business Casual
Majors: Business, Economics, Enginering, CS, and related majors
